Welcome to Residencia Martinez, we offer the ultimate boarding experience for college women.

Cozy Rooms, and Secure Spaces

Each room in our boarding house comfortably accommodates four students with bunk beds, wardrobes, a spacious desk, and chairs. Enjoy the luxury of private toilets and bathrooms in every room, ensuring convenience and privacy.

Enjoy seamless connectivity with our unlimited Wi-Fi service. Whether you're diving into online classes, researching, or streaming your favorite content, our high-speed Wi-Fi ensures a reliable and uninterrupted connection.

Your safety is our priority. Our boarding house is equipped with state-of-the-art CCTV cameras strategically placed to ensure a secure environment. Rest easy knowing that every corner is under watch, providing you with peace of mind.

We provide a convenient solution for all your document needs at a cost that won't break the bank. Skip the hassle of external printing services and enjoy the convenience of quality prints right at your fingertips.

Our boarding house features a dedicated water tank reservoir, ensuring a constant and ample water flow. Say goodbye to shortages – we prioritize your convenience, so you can focus on what matters most during your stay with us.
